The physical classroom learning nowadays is no longer applicable for the current younger generation. Internet and distance learning which is generally known as online education plays a vital role in the country’s education system. It is undeniable that online education provides ample of benefits to young learners So in this study we’re going to create an online platform for the people to use for the education, in which a person can search any course based on their interest and pursue with the course by using the video and course information that has been provided by the online course platform. Technology used for developing this platform, Frontend-Html, Css and Javascript and for backend we are using php.
